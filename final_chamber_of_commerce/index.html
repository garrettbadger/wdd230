Who We Are

The Meridian Chamber of Commerce is a membership organization, supported by member investment and the committed involvement of leaders in our business community. The Chamber provides ways to become involved in the community through leadership opportunities, business advocacy, networking, and promotion of individual businesses.

Formed in 1958, the Meridian Chamber of Commerce is the premiere business membership organization in Idaho's second largest city. As we approach 100,000 residents in the City of Meridian, the Meridian Chamber has grown to include large international corporations as well as small entrepreneur fueled business. We currently have over 600 members that belong to the Chamber, and while our population may grow, we will never stray from our mission to serve, strengthen and promote the Meridian business community.

Local Businesses

Idaho Central Credit Union

Idaho Central Credit Union is one of Idaho's most successful credit unions. It started out in Boise as a state chartered credit union back in 1940 to help serve the needs of the officers of some of Idaho's other credit unions. Today ICCU is proud to support Idahoans from 36 of Idahos counties. Idaho Central Credit Union currently serves more that 390,000 members and holds $5 billion in assets.

Vertical View

Vertical View is a new and state of the art climbing gym located in Meridian. It has lead climbing, top roping and bouldering all in one place with a wall that is 65 feet tall. It features a unique layout with stadium seating due to Meridian's building code which states that no commercial buildings can exceed 65 feet in height. This meant that during construction they needed to dig below the grounds surface. This wonderful gym also has a weight room and hot yoga studio with conference rooms and spots for private parties.

Commercial Tire

Commercial Tire is not unique to Idaho but it is one of Meridan's top tire places. Commercial tire is an employee owned compnay that specializes in all things tire. They do tire rotations, tire balancing, new tire installation, flat repair, wheel alignment and they even have services for brakes and oil changes. Commercial tire is located in the Northwest of the United States and we are proud to have them here in Meridian.
