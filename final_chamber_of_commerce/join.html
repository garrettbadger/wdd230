Non-profit Tier

The Non-pofit tier allows you to be a part of the Chamber of Commerce and receive all of the default benefits of joining the Chamber. These include things like added publicity on our website, direct contacts with other members of the Chamber, and a spot in the anuual dairy days parade.

Bronze Tier

The Bronze tier includes all of the benefits of the Non-profit Tier plus the inclusion of a directory listing on our webpage as well as a monthly spotlight in a local radio station.

Silver Tier

The Silver tier includes all of the benefits of the Non-profit tier and the Bronze tier plus some extra goodies. You now have access to social media posts advertising your site on our facebook and instagram pages. You will also be given increased networking oppotunities with local advertising companies.

Gold Tier

The Gold tier includes all of the benefits of all three previous tiers.As a member of the Gilver tier you are now eligible to attend our quartlery business trainings which are put on by members of the Chamber who are exceptionally successful and will share their knowledge and walk through scenarios to help you increase your business's success in our city.
