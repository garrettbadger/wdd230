Target Audience: My target audience for this website is mainly for adults who live in the South East part of Idaho. Especially for younger adults who are looking to meet people or take their kids to fun events.


Persona 1: Jake is a 26 year-old newly wed with a 2-year old daughter named Ashley. Him and his wife love to be involved in the community and know whats going on around them. They also like to be prepared and always make sure they check the forecast. What Jake and his wife need is a website that has it all, community, events, and weather.

Persona 2: Samantha is a 40 year-old single lady who has just recently moved to South Eastern Idaho and is looking to connect with her community. She loves to contribute to her community through service and loves to meet new people.

Scenario 1: Jake just found a sitter for his daugher Ashley and is looking for something fun to do on the weekend with his wife. He decides to check out seidahocommunity.com for any potential events that are happening this weekened. He sees that there is a country concert happening in a park only 5 minutes away from his house. Him and his wife have a great evening at a local concert with people from their community because of this website.

Scenario 2: Jake is worried that with fall approaching that the weather might be a little unpredictable for his camping trip that he has planned for over the weekend. Luckily seidahocommunity.com has weather for not just his town but the surrounding area where he will be camping so he can stay prepared.

Scenario 3: Samantha has extra time off of work this weekend and is looking for something fun to do. As she is looking at the weather on her local site she notices that under the events page there is a cultural festival this weekend with food and dance, so she decides to as it will be a good opportunity to meet people.

Scenario 4: Samantha has heard at work that there is going to be some changes to one of the city charters affecting public parks. She is interested in when she can go to the city council meeting and give her input on the changes. Luckliy for her seidahocommunity.com has a news section that tells her what has been going on in her community.

Scenario 5: Jake and his wife are eager to find something that they can do with their young daughter. After seeing that this weekend the weather is supposed to be nice they look for any community events happening. They see that there is an art in the park on Saturday and decide to go.
